While most classmates were focused on grades, I was running a small merchandise operation inside college — covering my own expenses, managing margins at 19, and discovering that building something from zero is more satisfying than almost anything else.

Venture 1 — D2C E-Commerce
Carpediem Studio

Co-founded a D2C brand selling handcrafted footwear at a time when that category had no market validation in India. We served 500+ customers across domestic and international markets. Shut it down two years later. The category went mainstream a decade later — we were just too early without a bridge strategy.

Being ahead of the market means nothing without a plan to bring your customer with you.

During COVID, when most people were waiting it out, I started a marble and granite export company. B2B, cross-border, zero prior domain experience. When the world pauses, founders find the gaps that were always there.
